    PREMIUM VILLA ROOM-VALLEY VIEW

    Valley Facing Villa Room

    These Villa rooms are right at the center of the property, Our Classic Villa Room is 49 sqm. It is a part of two bed room villa facing the valley. Definitely recommended for groups. Room offer a modern design with large king-size bed, mountain view sit out in room as well as living space outside the room. The guest can also enjoy nature with a access to private lawn.
    The superior rooms valley view are situated in the corner of a property in a ground plus three building. Being on the contours the main access to the building is from first floor, hence one has to climb only two floors without lift. The ground floor has a separate entry from ground floor. Its an boutique apartment building with large windows. The room is equipped with king size bed & Sofa cum bed, which can easily accommodate four adults. The room has a breathtaking view of mountains & valleys. Interiors are at par with any four star resort. The room is connected with one more room in case of a bigger family or a group. It consists of a main door, small lobby with two individual rooms facing each other. The room is equipped with LED tv, electric kettle and limited supply of Tea/Coffee/ Milk/Sugar sachets, hot water bath for guest convenience.
