    Comfortable rooms

    The rooms are situated in the corner of a property in a ground plus three building.
    Being on the contours the main access to the building is from first floor, hence one has to climb only two floors. The ground floor has a separate entry from ground floor. Its an boutique apartment building with large windows.
    The room is equipped with king size bed & Sofa cum bed, which can easily accommodate four adults. The room do not have a view but interiors are at par with any four star resort. The room is connected with one more room in case of a bigger family or a group. It consists of a main door, small lobby with two individual rooms facing each other. The room is equipped with LED tv, electric kettle and limited supply of Tea/Coffee/ Milk/Sugar sachets, hot water bath for guest convenience.
